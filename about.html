Our Story

Founded on the Belief That Better Information Builds Better Companies

NexPress was established in Washington, DC in 2014 by a group of former strategy consultants and financial analysts who had spent careers at the world's leading research and intelligence organizations. What they found, again and again, was that the best intelligence rarely reached the people who needed it most.

We built NexPress to change that. Starting with a weekly briefing that grew to 10,000 subscribers in its first year, we have expanded into a full-spectrum intelligence platform serving enterprise clients across 38 countries.

Today, NexPress publishes over 200 research reports annually and provides custom intelligence to some of the world's most significant institutions — all under a model of strict editorial independence.
